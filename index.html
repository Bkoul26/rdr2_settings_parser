Red Dead Redemption 2 system.xml Settings Parser v0.1

Red Dead Redemption 2 for PC has a lot of graphics settings. Taking screenshots is clunky, so why not parse that settings file into a nice text block?

Paste the contents of your RDR2 settings file (found at /Users/Username/Documents/Rockstar Games/Red Dead Redemption 2/Settings/system.xml) in the left area
and a nicely formatted settings block will appear on the right, ready to copy/paste into your favorite online discussion.

  • For feedback: Reddit, Github or @jbaert.
  • Good threads with settings and discussion: Guru3D, ResetEra, Reddit.
  • The game only saves your settings to system.xml on succesful exit.
